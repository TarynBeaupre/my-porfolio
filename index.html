About Me

Hi there!

I’m a developer passionate about crafting impactful solutions using modern technology. I'm currently based in Montreal, Canada, and completing a three year technical DEC in Computer Science at John Abbott College. I plan on attending university for Computer Science as well.

From a young age, I have been fascinated with the inner workings of electronics and the incredible innovative potential of technology. My childhood curiosity has since culminated in a love for programming and development. In my spare time, I love to play video games, do art and spend time with my two lovely pets and my family. I also run a small business that I founded in March 2023. I handmake all the jewelry I sell, and it's been a really good creative outlet. It's grown to a sizeable business at 100k+ followers across social media.

In summer 2024, I interned at Bell as a cybersecurity developer intern. During this internship I was lucky enough to be given the freedom to develop a new feature for the team, with guidance from a senior developer. This was my first internship and my first experience working in the field as a professional. I learned so much.

This spring 2025, I will be doing a development stage at UDEM where I'll be working on a multiple of educational projects. I have a thirst for adventure, so I can't wait to see what's next!
